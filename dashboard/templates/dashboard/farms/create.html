About Farms

A farm represents a specific agricultural unit managed by a farmer. Farms can be:

  • Private: Owned and managed by individual farmers
  • Cooperative: Jointly owned and managed by a group
  • Leased: Rented land for agricultural use
  • Family: Passed down through generations
  • Communal: Shared community land

After creating a farm, you can add production data, yield records, labor information, and track farm inputs.
