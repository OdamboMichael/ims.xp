About Farmers

Farmers are the primary users of the system. Each farmer can have multiple farms and can belong to multiple clusters.

  • Farmer ID: Unique identifier for each farmer (e.g., FARM-001)
  • National ID: Government-issued identification number
  • Years of Farming: Experience in agricultural activities
  • Location: Current operational area for farming activities
  • Residence: Where the farmer lives (may differ from farm location)

After adding a farmer, you can assign farms to them and add them to clusters for better management.
